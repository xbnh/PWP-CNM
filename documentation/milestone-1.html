Milestone 2!

Milestone 1: Personal Website Project:

Purpose:

The purpose of this personal website project is to showcase my portfolio as a web developer and to provide potential clients and employers with an overview of my skills and projects!

Audience:

The audience for this site includes potential employers, freelance clients, and fellow developers interested in my work and skills in web development

Goal:

The primary goal of this website is to secure freelance clients and full-time employment opportunities by showcasing my projects, skills, and experience in web development

Persona:

Emily W. Carter | Age: 29

Profession:

Marketing Director at EcoTrend, a growing startup focused on sustainable fashion. Emily is responsible for building the company’s online presence and is currently seeking a freelance web developer to redesign the company’s website and improve its user experience.

Technology:

Emily is an Apple enthusiast who uses a MacBook Air for work and an iPhone 12 Pro for mobile tasks. She is familiar with project management tools like Trello and Asana and prefers using cloud-based collaboration software like Google Workspace. She is always connected and uses her phone to check emails and manage projects while on the go.

Attitudes & Behaviors:

Emily is detail-oriented and prioritizes aesthetics and user experience. She has a busy work schedule and often multitasks, so she prefers websites that are easy to navigate and offer quick access to portfolios and contact information. Emily typically spends about 5-7 minutes evaluating developer portfolios and focuses on the design, layout, and user flow.

Frustrations & Needs:

Emily is looking for a web developer who shares EcoTrend’s values of sustainability and understands the importance of visually appealing, eco-friendly design. She is frustrated by portfolios that are outdated or lack details about the developer’s process and contributions. She needs someone who can balance creativity with functionality, is easy to communicate with, and can meet tight deadlines.

Goals:

Emily’s goal is to find a freelance web developer who can redesign EcoTrend’s website to improve user engagement, increase online sales, and effectively communicate the company’s brand values. She needs to quickly assess whether a candidate has the right balance of design skill, technical expertise, and understanding of sustainability.

User Story:

As a user, I need to determine if this developer can create an aesthetically pleasing and functional website that aligns with EcoTrend’s brand and mission.
