Milestone 1: Personal Website Project:

Purpose:

The purpose of this personal website project is to showcase my portfolio as a web developer and to provide potential clients and employers with an overview of my skills and projects!

Audience:

The audience for this site includes potential employers, freelance clients, and fellow developers interested in my work and skills in web development

Goal:

The primary goal of this website is to secure freelance clients and full-time employment opportunities by showcasing my projects, skills, and experience in web development

Persona:

Name: Sarah Johnson

Sarah is a 30-year-old project manager at a tech company looking for web developers to build responsive, modern websites for her clients. She uses her smartphone and laptop to browse developer portfolios and seeks someone who can communicate effectively and deliver projects on time. Sarah is frustrated by developers wth incomplete or outdated portfolios and wants to find someone whose works aligns with her company's style and values. Her goal is to find a reliable developer she can trust for ongoing freelance projects.
